The Pylon

A symbolic structure representing the university's core values: Truth, Excellence, and Wisdom. It is a popular landmark and photo spot at the main entrance.

The Mural

A historical art piece showcasing PUP's commitment to nation-building and social responsibility. It often highlights Filipino heritage and student activism.

Visitor's Lounge

A designated waiting and receiving area for campus guests. It ensures a comfortable space for visitors while administrative processes are being arranged.

Open Court

An outdoor multi-use area for sports, events, and student activities. It's often used for recreational play and organizational events.

Lawn Tennis Court

Dedicated courts for tennis training and tournaments. PE classes and varsity practices are regularly held here.

Basketball Court

A well-used space for student athletes, PE classes, and inter-college competitions. It's one of the most active sports areas on campus.

Souvenir Shop

Offers official PUP merchandise like shirts, IDs, and accessories. A favorite stop for students and alumni.

A historical site dedicated to Apolinario Mabini, the 'Brains of the Revolution.' It stands as a tribute to his intellect and patriotism.

Mabini Museum

Contains documents, artifacts, and exhibits about Apolinario Mabini's life and works. It serves both educational and heritage functions.

Obelisk

A tall monument that adds to the historic atmosphere of the Mabini area. It symbolizes honor and legacy.

A multi-denominational worship space for spiritual activities, reflection, and religious services. It supports inclusivity for all faiths.

Main Academic Building

One of the central buildings for general education and major-specific classes. It houses several classrooms and administrative offices.

Gazebo

Small shaded structures scattered around campus for study, group work, or breaks. Often used by student organizations for informal meetings.

The Grandstand

The athletic track and field for running and various outdoor sports. Used heavily by student athletes and for PE.

Sampaguita Building

Student Center

A hub for student services, organizations, and council activities. It hosts meetings, programs, and organizations operations.

Charlie del Rosario Building

Named after a former student leader. It contains classrooms and often serves general academic purposes.

Linear Park

A landscaped path surrounded by greenery. Used for walking, relaxing, or informal group gatherings.

Laboratory High School Building

The facility for PUP's laboratory high school. Offers junior and senior high school classes with hands-on training.

Printing Press Building

Where university publications and academic printing needs are processed. It supports both academic and administrative units.

Manages logistics, equipment, and supplies for the university. Ensures smooth internal operations.

The main library of PUP. Offers vast academic resources, reading areas, and research support.
